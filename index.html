About Me

Abhinav Bhati is a senior Computer Science student at the University of Western Ontario, passionate about the ever-evolving world of artificial intelligence. His academic path has allowed him to dive deep into the fundamentals of software engineering, algorithms, and machine learning, while building a mindset focused on innovation and experimentation.

Outside the classroom, Abhinav is always seeking a balance between creativity and discipline. Whether he’s exploring new destinations or pushing through reps at the gym, he finds inspiration in movement and challenge — values that also reflect in his approach to solving problems through code.

He’s especially fascinated by the intersection of AI and gaming. This has led him to develop artificial agents capable of playing games, a personal passion that fuses strategy, data, and behavior modeling into one immersive playground for learning.

Over time, Abhinav has discovered the value of iteration — not just in his code, but in himself. Through trial and reflection, he’s grown into a curious, self-driven learner who’s always eager to take on new projects and refine his skills. You can follow his journey, experiments, and creations throughout this site.
